About

My name is Ismaeel Zakariya. I grew up in Kerala and am currently based there. I've been into photography and videography since childhood.

I believe in design and communication as powerful tools to solve problems and make a positive impact on people's minds.

I have solid experience in this field and I've delivered work to happy clients — even though I'm new to freelancing.

I love my work and I deliver it with my personal experience and realization, blended with the client's imagination and a strong storytelling approach.
